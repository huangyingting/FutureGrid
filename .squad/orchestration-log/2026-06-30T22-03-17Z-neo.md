## BarChartRace Implementation (#29)

### Approach

1. Created `components/charts/BarChartRace.tsx` (client, d3-based)
2. Data source: BLS OEWS multi-year employment data (2019–2025)
3. Layout: 15 bars × (28px height + 6px gap) + margins → ~524px tall; 700ms viewBox width
4. Animation engine: D3 transitions (700ms ease-cubic-in-out) for bar width + y-position
5. Controls: Play/Pause/Replay buttons + year scrubber (manual override)
6. Auto-advance: 1.2s/year during playback; stops at end; Replay restarts
7. Reduced-motion: Animations disabled; scrubber still functional; Play button disabled
8. Color: Consistent with QuadrantScatterChart (colorForRisk automation probability)
9. i18n: 10 keys added to explore.ts (raceTitle, raceSubhead, racePlay, racePause, raceReplay, raceYear, raceEmployment, raceRank, raceOccupation, raceNoData)

- `components/charts/BarChartRace.tsx`

- Extended `lib/i18n/messages/zh/explore.ts` (10 keys)

### Decisions

1. **Animation engine:** D3-only (framer-motion not installed)
