- **Fix:** Added persistent "About this data" disclaimer near hero stats: "*Automation risk estimates are based on Frey & Osborne (2013), a research model. These are probabilistic estimates, not official forecasts or guarantees.*"
- **Result:** Methodology discoverable, limitations disclosed.
